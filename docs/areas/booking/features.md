### 1. Smart Scheduling

- [Details](./features/smart-scheduling.md)

### 2. Schedule Template Builder
Create and manage pre-configured day/week templates with color-coded appointment slots.
- [Details](./features/schedule-template-builder.md)

**Key Capabilities**:
- Create day and week templates with pre-defined time blocks
- Assign appointment types to slots (Scan, First Appointment, Adjustment, etc.)
- Color coding and icon assignment for visual identification
- Pre-assign chairs/rooms and providers to slots
- Apply templates to dates, date ranges, or recurring patterns
- Smart booking into template placeholders
- Template analytics and utilization tracking
- AI-powered template optimization suggestions
- Seasonal and provider-specific templates
- Multi-location template sharing
