# Compliance & Documentation - Features

## Feature List

### 1. Regulatory Compliance Manager
Comprehensive tracking and management of HIPAA, PIPEDA, OSHA, and state board requirements.
- [Details](./features/regulatory-compliance-manager.md)

**Key Capabilities**:
- HIPAA privacy and security rule compliance
- PIPEDA personal information protection
- OSHA safety program management
- CDC infection control guidelines
- State dental board regulations
- Risk assessment tools
- Audit trail maintenance

### 2. Consent Form System
Digital consent form management with electronic signatures.
- [Details](./features/consent-form-system.md)

**Key Capabilities**:
- Treatment-specific consent forms
- Digital signature capture
- Form versioning and updates
- Expiration tracking and renewals
- HIPAA privacy notices
- Financial responsibility agreements
- Medical history questionnaires

### 3. Clinical Protocols Library
Repository of clinical protocols and procedure checklists.
- [Details](./features/clinical-protocols-library.md)

**Key Capabilities**:
- Daily operational checklists (opening/closing)
- Clinical procedure protocols
- Emergency preparedness procedures
- Photo and imaging protocols
- Equipment safety checks
- Sterilization logs
- Protocol compliance tracking

### 4. Staff Training & Certification Tracker
Manage staff training, certifications, and continuing education.
- [Details](./features/staff-training-tracker.md)

**Key Capabilities**:
- New hire orientation tracking
- License and certification management
- Expiration alerts and renewal reminders
- Continuing education tracking
- Training completion documentation
- CPR/BLS, X-ray, HIPAA, OSHA certification
- Certificate document storage

### 5. Incident Reporting System
Document and track clinical incidents, safety issues, and protocol deviations.
- [Details](./features/incident-reporting-system.md)

**Key Capabilities**:
- Incident reporting forms
- Investigation workflow
- Corrective action tracking
- Protocol deviation logging
- Trend analysis
- Supervisor review and approval
- Reporting to regulatory authorities

### 6. Audit Preparation Tools
Streamline internal and external audit preparation and management.
- [Details](./features/audit-preparation-tools.md)

**Key Capabilities**:
- Self-audit checklists
- Document compilation for audits
- Compliance evidence gathering
- Audit trail reports
- Finding and corrective action tracking
- Mock audit support

### 7. Safety Data Library
Centralized repository for safety data sheets and hazardous materials management.
- [Details](./features/safety-data-library.md)

**Key Capabilities**:
- Safety data sheets (SDS) for all chemicals
- SDS expiration tracking
- Easy staff access
- Hazardous waste disposal tracking
- Disposal log maintenance
- Vendor compliance tracking
