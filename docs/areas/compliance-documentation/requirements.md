# Compliance & Documentation - Requirements

## Overview
The Compliance & Documentation module ensures regulatory compliance (HIPAA, PIPEDA, OSHA, state boards) and manages required documentation, consent forms, clinical protocols, and safety procedures.

## Goals
- Ensure full regulatory compliance (HIPAA, PIPEDA, OSHA)
- Maintain required documentation and audit trails
- Manage consent forms and patient agreements
- Track staff training and certifications
- Document clinical protocols and procedures
- Prepare for regulatory audits
- Minimize legal and regulatory risk
