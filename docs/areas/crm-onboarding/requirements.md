# CRM & Patient Onboarding - Requirements

## Overview
The CRM & Patient Onboarding module manages the entire new patient intake process and ongoing relationship management.

## Goals
- Streamline new patient onboarding
- Centralize patient demographic and contact information
- Track patient communication history
- Manage referral sources and relationships

## Requirements

### Patient Information Management
- [ ] Capture complete patient demographics
- [ ] Store emergency contact information
- [ ] Manage insurance information
- [ ] Track referral sources
- [ ] Record patient preferences and notes

### Communication Tracking
- [ ] Log all patient communications (calls, emails, messages)
- [ ] Track appointment reminders sent
- [ ] Maintain communication history timeline
- [ ] Support multiple contact methods per patient

### Onboarding Workflow
- [ ] Initial inquiry/contact capture
- [ ] New patient forms (digital)
- [ ] Consultation scheduling
- [ ] Medical history collection
- [ ] Insurance verification
- [ ] Treatment plan presentation
- [ ] Case acceptance tracking

### Relationship Management
- [ ] Track patient lifecycle stages
- [ ] Monitor patient engagement
- [ ] Identify at-risk patients
- [ ] Referral tracking and management
