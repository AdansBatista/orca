Summary
This file outlines the major features of the Patient Communications area at Booking-level detail.

- Messaging Hub: unified API to send SMS, Email, and In-App messages with routing, templates, and provider adapters.
- Patient Portal: secure patient authentication, profile management, appointment booking, results access, and billing payments.
- Automated Campaigns: campaign builder, triggers, schedules, A/B testing, and analytics.
- Educational Content Library: curated patient resources, personalization, scheduling, and delivery.
- Notification Preferences: per-patient channel preferences and frequency controls.
- Template Management: versioned templates with variable schemas and preview.
- Delivery & Logging: message history, delivery receipts, failure handling, and retry policies.
- Analytics & AI: send-time optimization, personalization, and sentiment analysis on feedback.
# Patient Communications - Features

## Feature List

### 1. Two-Way Messaging Hub
Unified communication platform for all patient interactions across channels.
- [Details](./features/two-way-messaging-hub.md)

**Key Capabilities**:
- SMS, Email, and secure in-app messaging
- Unified inbox with conversation threading
- Staff assignment and routing
- Message templates and canned responses
- File attachments and media sharing
- Bulk messaging to patient segments
- Message scheduling and automation

### 2. Patient Portal
Self-service portal enabling 24/7 patient access and reducing administrative workload.
- [Details](./features/patient-portal.md)

**Key Capabilities**:
- Online appointment booking and management
- View treatment progress and photos
- Make payments and view statements
- Upload insurance cards and documents
- Secure messaging with practice
- Access educational content
- Family account management
- Mobile-responsive design

### 3. Automated Communication Campaigns
Intelligent automation for routine patient communications.
- [Details](./features/automated-campaigns.md)

**Key Capabilities**:
- Post-appointment follow-ups
- Treatment milestone celebrations
- Appointment reminders (SMS, Email, Voice)
- Birthday and anniversary messages
- Re-engagement campaigns for inactive patients
- Payment reminders and confirmations
- Satisfaction surveys
- Trigger-based automation workflows

### 4. Educational Content Library

**Key Capabilities**:
- Treatment phase-specific education

**Key Capabilities**:
