# Resource & Staff Management

Purpose
Coordinate chairs/rooms/equipment and staff assignments to maximize utilization and reduce idle time.

Primary Users
- Practice managers, operations coordinators, resource schedulers

Core Functions
- Resource Inventory & Status: chairs, rooms, devices, maintenance state.
- Staff Roster & Availability: clock-in/out, breaks, skills/certifications mapping.
- Assignment Engine: suggested staff-resource-appointment matching.
- Swap & Break Handling: temporary reassignment and automated backfilling.
- Resource Maintenance & Alerts: schedule maintenance windows and flag faults.
- Shift Handoff & Handover Notes: capture active tasks and patient statuses at shift change.

Implementation Notes
- Integrate with HR/Staff Management and Resources Management areas for authoritative data.
- Provide override UI for manual assignment and bulk operations for managers.
