# Resources Management - Requirements

## Overview
The Resources Management module handles equipment, rooms, materials inventory, and asset tracking.

## Goals
- Optimize resource utilization
- Maintain equipment and inventory
- Prevent resource conflicts
- Track asset lifecycle

## Requirements

### Equipment Management
- [ ] Equipment catalog and tracking
- [ ] Maintenance schedules
- [ ] Repair history
- [ ] Equipment assignment and location
- [ ] Depreciation tracking

### Room Management
- [ ] Chair/operatory tracking
- [ ] Room availability scheduling
- [ ] Room setup and configuration
- [ ] Equipment per room tracking

### Inventory Management
- [ ] Materials and supplies catalog
- [ ] Stock level tracking
- [ ] Reorder alerts
- [ ] Inventory consumption tracking
- [ ] Expiration date monitoring

### Asset Tracking
- [ ] Asset register
- [ ] Purchase information
- [ ] Warranty tracking
- [ ] Asset lifecycle management
- [ ] Disposal and replacement planning

### Sterilization Tracking
- [ ] Instrument sterilization logs
- [ ] Sterilization cycle tracking
- [ ] Compliance documentation
- [ ] Equipment validation
