# Staff Management - Requirements

## Overview
The Staff Management module handles team coordination, scheduling, roles, and HR functions.

## Goals
- Streamline staff scheduling and coordination
- Manage roles and permissions
- Track staff performance
- Maintain HR records

## Requirements

### Staff Profiles
- [ ] Employee demographic information
- [ ] Role and position management
- [ ] Credentials and certifications tracking
- [ ] Emergency contact information
- [ ] Employment history

### Schedule Management
- [ ] Staff shift scheduling
- [ ] Time-off requests and approvals
- [ ] Coverage management
- [ ] Overtime tracking
- [ ] Schedule conflicts detection

### Roles & Permissions
- [ ] Role-based access control
- [ ] Custom role creation
- [ ] Permission assignment
- [ ] Multi-location access management

### Performance Tracking
- [ ] Task completion tracking
- [ ] Performance metrics
- [ ] Goal setting and monitoring
- [ ] Review cycles

### HR Functions
- [ ] Document management (contracts, certifications)
- [ ] Payroll preparation data
- [ ] Benefits administration
- [ ] Compliance tracking
