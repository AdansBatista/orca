# Treatment Management - Requirements

## Overview
The Treatment Management module handles the complete patient treatment lifecycle from initial consultation through retention.

## Goals
- Track treatment progress systematically
- Ensure treatment plan adherence
- Document clinical procedures
- Monitor treatment outcomes

## Requirements

### Treatment Planning
- [ ] Initial consultation documentation
- [ ] Treatment plan creation
- [ ] Multiple treatment option presentation
- [ ] Case acceptance tracking
- [ ] Treatment phase management

### Clinical Documentation
- [ ] Progress notes for each visit
- [ ] Procedure documentation
- [ ] Clinical findings and observations
- [ ] Treatment modifications tracking
- [ ] Appliance tracking (type, installation, adjustments)

### Treatment Tracking
- [ ] Treatment timeline visualization
- [ ] Phase completion tracking
- [ ] Appointment history per treatment
- [ ] Treatment milestones
- [ ] Expected vs actual progress comparison

### Orthodontic-Specific Features
- [ ] Bracket placement tracking
- [ ] Wire progression tracking
- [ ] Elastics and accessories management
- [ ] Retention phase management
- [ ] Debond date tracking

### Clinical Measurements
- [ ] Record clinical measurements (overjet, overbite, etc.)
- [ ] Track changes over time
- [ ] Before/during/after comparisons
- [ ] Treatment outcome assessment
