# Vendors Management - Requirements

## Overview
The Vendors Management module handles supplier relationships, procurement, and vendor performance tracking.

## Goals
- Streamline vendor relationships
- Optimize procurement processes
- Track vendor performance
- Manage contracts and agreements

## Requirements

### Vendor Profiles
- [ ] Vendor contact information
- [ ] Product/service catalog per vendor
- [ ] Vendor categorization
- [ ] Payment terms and conditions
- [ ] Tax information

### Purchase Orders
- [ ] Create and manage purchase orders
- [ ] PO approval workflow
- [ ] Order tracking and status
- [ ] Receiving and fulfillment
- [ ] Partial deliveries handling

### Vendor Performance
- [ ] Delivery time tracking
- [ ] Quality ratings
- [ ] Price competitiveness analysis
- [ ] Vendor scorecards
- [ ] Issue and dispute tracking

### Contract Management
- [ ] Contract storage and retrieval
- [ ] Contract renewal reminders
- [ ] Terms and conditions tracking
- [ ] Pricing agreements
- [ ] Service level agreements (SLAs)

### Procurement Analytics
- [ ] Spending analysis by vendor
- [ ] Purchase history and trends
- [ ] Cost savings opportunities
- [ ] Vendor comparison reports
